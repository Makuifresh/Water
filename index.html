About Makui Fresh

Makui Fresh Natural Spring Water is sourced from the pristine hills of Somdal Village, Ukhrul District, Manipur.

Every drop is carefully purified and hygienically bottled to preserve freshness, purity and natural goodness.

Our commitment is to provide safe, refreshing and high-quality drinking water while preserving the natural mineral balance of our spring source.
