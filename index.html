About Makui Fresh

Makui Fresh Natural Spring Water is sourced from the pristine hills of Somdal Village, Ukhrul District, Manipur. Every drop is carefully purified and hygienically bottled to preserve its refreshing taste, purity and natural goodness.

Our mission is to provide safe, refreshing and high-quality drinking water while preserving the natural mineral balance of our spring source.
